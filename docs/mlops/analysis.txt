The prototype is done.

Running on a laptop w/ 32 GB RAM and 2.8 GHZ processor with 4 cores
and using 1 processer, 1 HPO trial takes 1.75 min using a train dataset
size of 1000 and validation dataset size of 1000.
Each subsequent trial takes 1 minute each.
10 trials is about 11 minutes.

scaling up to 463,000 train samples and 67,000 validation for 10 trials
would roughly be ((11 min * 463)/60.)/24. = 3.54 days
Using the Kaggle P100, T4, or L4 GPUs could reduce the time by a factor
of roughly 8, making the HPO phase about 11 hours.
the train phase = about 2 hours.
the test phase = about 20 minutes.
