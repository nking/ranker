The prototype is done.

Running on a laptop w/ 32 GB RAM and 2.8 GHZ processor with 4 cores
and using 1 processer, 1 HPO trial takes 1.75 min using a train dataset
size of 1000 and validation dataset size of 1000.
scaling up to 20 trials and 463,0000 train samples and 67,000 validation
samples would roughly be ((1.75*20*463)/60.)/24. = 11 days.
Using the Kaggle P100, T4, or L4 GPUs could reduce the time by a factor
of roughly 8, making the HPO phase almost a day and a half.
