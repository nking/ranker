google grain SPMD model shards the data across each jax process
  - each jax process gets 1/(jax.process_count()) of all data.
  - the data is further sharded among local_devices by
    code in train.py

The model is not sharded.  Programmatically, the gradients and loss are synchronized
across all devices, not just local devices, in order to update the
model and optimizer.
